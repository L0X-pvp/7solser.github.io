We're proud to share with you our short film Croak, which follows the adventures of one lonesome frog on a quest to find love. Produced in-house as one of our treasured passion projects, it was directed and animated by Frida.

The story takes us on a journey from the desert to the rainforest and beyond as our frog goes in search of a mate, but ends up looking for love in all the wrong places. The idea began life as a short loop based on just one scene, but Frida was keen to explore the possibility of turning the vignette into a full story. So, we started talking about how the animation could become a short film.
